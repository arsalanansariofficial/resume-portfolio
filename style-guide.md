### Style Guide

## Break Points

```
1.  640 px - Small       -  sm
2.  768 px - Medium      -  md
3. 1024 px - Large       -  lg
4. 1280 px - Extra Large -  xl
5. 1536 px - 2x Large    - 2xl
```
